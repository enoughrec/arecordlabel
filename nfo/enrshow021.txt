[EN] Enough Records Radio Show, curated by the label's head ps, one hour of sounds from the Enough Records eclectic and extensive back-catalogue. This episode focused on different electro sounds, with a short selection of tracks by our artist Chuzausen near the end. Broadcast first at <a href="https://futuremusic.fm/">FutureMusic.FM</a> on 8th of July 2018. Photo by <a href="https://flic.kr/p/7bskvc">Chris "wunztwice"</a>. Re-broadcast at <a href="https://radiomanobras.pt">Radio Manobras</a> with Portuguese voice over on 22nd of July 2018 (<a href="https://www.mixcloud.com/enoughrecords/enough-records-radio-show-021-pt/">mixcloud link</a>).
<br /><br />
[PT] Programa de radio Enough Records, curado por ps, cabeçilha regente da editora. Com uma hora de sons retirados do catálogo da Enough e com alguns convidados ocasionais. Este episódio focado em sons mais electro, com uma seleção de temas do nosso artista Chuzausen mais no final. Transmitido originalmente na <a href="https://futuremusic.fm/">FutureMusic.FM</a> no dia 8 de Julho de 2018. Foto de <a href="https://flic.kr/p/7bskvc">Chris "wunztwice"</a>. Retransmitido na <a href="https://radiomanobras.pt">Radio Manobras</a> com voz off Portuguesa em 22 de Julho de 2018 (<a href="https://www.mixcloud.com/enoughrecords/enough-records-radio-show-021-pt/">mixcloud link</a>).
