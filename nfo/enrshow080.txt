[EN] Enough Records Radio Show, curated by ps, two hours of sounds taken from Enough Records eclectic and extensive back-catalogue. Special episode featuring the Mixed Drone Music Competition from <a href="https://2022.inercia.pt">Inércia Demoparty 2022</a> which Enough Records helped organize. This episode premiered on November 2022 and was broadcast for the first time at <a href="https://radioquantica.com">Radio Quântica</a>, <a href="https://radiomanobras.pt">Radio Manobras</a>, <a href="https://piratona.alg-a.org/">Radio Piratona</a>, <a href="https://www.internetpublicradio.live/">Internet Public Radio</a> and <a href="https://dfm.nu/">DFM RTV INT</a>.
<br /><br />
[PT] Programa de radio da Enough Records, curado por ps, cabeçilha regente da editora. Com duas horas de sons retirados do catálogo da Enough. Episódio especial com inclusão das músicas submetidas à competição de música drone da <a href="https://2022.inercia.pt">Inércia Demoparty 2022</a> que a Enough Records ajudou a organizar. Programa transmitido pela primera vez em Novembro 2022 na <a href="https://radioquantica.com">Radio Quântica</a>, <a href="https://radiomanobras.pt">Radio Manobras</a>, <a href="https://piratona.alg-a.org/">Radio Piratona</a>, <a href="https://www.internetpublicradio.live/">Internet Public Radio</a> e <a href="https://dfm.nu/">DFM RTV INT</a>.
