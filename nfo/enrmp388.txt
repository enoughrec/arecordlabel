[EN] 9 years after his debut EP on Enough Records, <a href="https://soundcloud.com/violet-and-the-mutants">Violet and The Mutants</a> have finally finished their debut album, 11 tracks of electro industrial by Daniel Carvalho and friends. Including collaboration tracks with <a href="https://timeshiftermusic.bandcamp.com/releases">Time Shifter</a> and Psyjar. Artwork by <a href="https://www.behance.net/helder-costa">Hélder Costa</a>.
<br /><br />
[PT] 9 anos depois do seu EP de estreia, <a href="https://soundcloud.com/violet-and-the-mutants">Violet and The Mutants</a> regressa às edições com um album de 11 temas de electrónica industrial produzida por Daniel Carvalho com colaborações de <a href="https://timeshiftermusic.bandcamp.com/releases">Time Shifter</a> e Psyjar. Grafismo de <a href="https://www.behance.net/helder-costa">Hélder Costa</a>.
