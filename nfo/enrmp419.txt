[EN] Short cinematic / IDM / mashup / ambient remix album by our staff member <a href="http://tpolm.org/~ps">ps</a> featuring tracks and samples from the album "Lost Holograms" by <a href="https://soundcloud.com/marcel-gherman-megatone">Megatone</a>; a bunch of samples taken from freesound.org made by dobroide, flcellogrl, inspectorj, kangaroovindaloo, kvgarlic; and sampling from the movies Zero Theorem, Reservoir Dogs and Dead Pool. Photo by <a href="https://www.pexels.com/photo/bridge-cars-evening-expressway-344544/">Amiroo Hosseini</a>.
<br /><br />
[PT] EP mashup remistura de sonoridades cinemáticas / IDM / ambiental pelas mãos do nosso membro de staff member <a href="http://tpolm.org/~ps">ps</a> usando temas e samples retiradas do album "Lost Holograms" de <a href="https://soundcloud.com/marcel-gherman-megatone">Megatone</a>; um punhado de samples do freesound.org gravadas por dobroide, flcellogrl, inspectorj, kangaroovindaloo, kvgarlic; e samplando os filmes Zero Theorem, Reservoir Dogs e Dead Pool. Foto por <a href="https://www.pexels.com/photo/bridge-cars-evening-expressway-344544/">Amiroo Hosseini</a>.
