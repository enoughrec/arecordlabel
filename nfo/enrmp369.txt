[EN] Electronic album by Portuguese project <a href="https://soundcloud.com/dunekan">Sci Fi Industries</a>, bundling different remix and collaboration tracks he has worked on during the past few years. Featuring collaboration works with U.M.M., Xotox, shhh..., Mikroben Krieg, Broto Verbo, Himalayha, Sektor 304, Human Being, Yoshihiro Kikuchi, Abuka, Calhau and Waste Disposal Machine.
<br /><br />
[PT] Album de música electrónica pelas mãos do projecto Português <a href="https://soundcloud.com/dunekan">Sci Fi Industries</a> erradicado nos Estados Unidos. Desta feita com 10 temas de colaborações e remisturas feitas com diferentes projectos ao longo dos últimos anos. Inclui temas com U.M.M., Xotox, shhh..., Mikroben Krieg, Broto Verbo, Himalayha, Sektor 304, Human Being, Yoshihiro Kikuchi, Abuka, Calhau and Waste Disposal Machine.
