ps - Enough Records Radio Show #001 [enrshow001]

[EN] Enough Records Radio Show, curated by the label's head ps, expect one hour of sounds from the Enough Records eclectic and extensive back-catalogue. Also featuring occasional guest mixtapes and random hidden gems found straight from the underground. Broadcast first at <a href="https://futuremusic.fm/">FutureMusic.FM</a> on 17 of September 2017. Photo by <a href="https://flic.kr/p/4EZQZQ">Sandy Poore</a>. Re-broadcast at <a href="https://radiomanobras.pt">Radio Manobras</a> with Portuguese voice over on 15 of October 2017 (<a href="https://www.mixcloud.com/enoughrecords/enough-records-radio-show-001-pt/">mixcloud link</a>).
