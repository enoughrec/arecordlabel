[EN] Mashup by an unknown artist, using samples from old movies and music albums to compose a concept album about the possibility of an upcoming american civil war. Promoting global critical thinking independent media sites such as <a href="http://infowars.com">infowars.com</a>. Part of our <a href="http://www.enoughrecords.scene.org/anonymous_archives/">Anonymous Archives</a> sub-label.

<br /><br />
[PT] Album de mashups pelas mãos de um artista desconhecido, utilizando sampling de filmes e albums antigos para compor um album conceptual sobre uma nova possivel guerra civil americana. Promovendo uma cultura de pensamento critico dos media independentes tais como o website <a href="http://infowars.com">infowars.com</a>. Parte do catálogo da nossa sub-label <a href="http://www.enoughrecords.scene.org/anonymous_archives/">Anonymous Archives</a>.
