Konstantin Elfimov - Unlimited Pieces
<br /><br />
<img src="http://enoughrecords.scene.org/covers/enrmp488.jpg" />
<br /><br />
[EN] Full-length experimental album by Russian artist Konstantin Elfimov exploring limits of perception with this long form generative mashup piece layering snippets of multiple sources with incredible depth and attention to emerging undertones underneath the persistent chaos. Artwork by <a href="https://www.facebook.com/daniel.mcmahan.923">Daniel Mcmahan</a>.
<br /><br />
[PT] Album experimental do artista Russo Konstantin Elfimov, explorando os limites da percepção com um album de sonoridades generativas compostas por várias camadas de fontes distintas misturadas com incrivel profundidade e atenção aos tons emergentes que surgem no meio do caos persistente. Grafismo de <a href="https://www.facebook.com/daniel.mcmahan.923">Daniel Mcmahan</a>.
