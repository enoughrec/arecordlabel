[EN] Fourth release of a series of mixtapes raising awareness to the Anonymous, #antisec, Wikileaks and Occupy Wall Street movements. This fourth release also focuses on SOPA / PIPA, ACTA and #PL118. Organized in colaboration with <a href="https://xdatelier.org">xDA hackerspace</a>, this release is part of Enough Records social and political activist sub-label Anonymous Archives and has it's own <a href="https://enoughrecords.scene.org/anonymous_archives/">minipage</a>. Mixed by ps. Cover by Roland Henry.<br>
