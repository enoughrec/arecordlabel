Zé Trigueiros - Bear With Me [enrmp384]

[EN] Drummer of the band dUASsEMICOLCHEIASiNVERTIDAS and noise therapist of the A.T.R. collective, <a href="https://soundcloud.com/zetrigueiros">Zé Trigueiros</a> shows us for the first time a set of homemade songs, created in recent years between trips and stops… A melancholic instrumental work, which has the collaboration of the musician and luthier <a href="https://www.facebook.com/humonkeyinstruments/">Domenikos de Visser</a> on the bass and tzouras, “Bear With Me” was recorded in 2014/15 at his home in Amsterdam.

[PT] Baterista da banda dUASsEMICOLCHEIASiNVERTIDAS e terapista do ruido do colectivo A.T.R., <a href="https://soundcloud.com/zetrigueiros">Zé Trigueiros</a> lança o seu album de instrumentais melancolicos e cinemáticos, em colaboração com <a href="https://www.facebook.com/humonkeyinstruments/">Domenikos de Visser</a> no baixo e tzouras. "Bear With Me" foi rgavado em 2014 e 2015 na sua casa em Amsterdão.
