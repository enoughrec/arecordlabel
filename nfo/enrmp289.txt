[EN] Lolicore album by US project No Loli-Gagging. Mixing influences from hardcore, breakcore, speedcore, gabber, trance, drum n bass, techno, house, noise and even ambient. All with the usual heavy dosage of high pitched lolita vocals and abrupt track endings.

[PT] Album de lolicore pelas mãos do projecto americano No Loli-Gagging. Misturando influências do hardcore, breakcore, speedcore, gabber, trance, drum n bass, techno, house, noise e até ambient. Tudo com a tipica dose elevada de vozes aceleradas de lolitas, em temas de curta duração com finais súbitos.
