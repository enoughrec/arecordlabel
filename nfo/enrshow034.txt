[EN] Enough Records Radio Show, curated by ps, two hours of sounds taken from Enough Records eclectic and extensive back-catalogue. This episode premiered on May 2019 and was broadcast at <a href="https://radioquantica.com">Rádio Quântica</a>, <a href="https://radiomanobras.pt">Rádio Manobras</a> and <a href="https://piratona.alg-a.org/">Radio Piratona</a>. Photo by <a href="https://flic.kr/p/eU1DMU">James H.</a>. On this episode we do a special retrospective show focusing on two compilations co-organized with <a href="https://thisco.bandcamp.com/">Thisco Records</a>.
<br /><br />
[PT] Programa de radio Enough Records, curado por ps, cabeçilha regente da editora. Com duas horas de sons retirados do catálogo da Enough. Transmitido em Maio 2019 na <a href="https://radioquantica.com">Rádio Quântica</a>, <a href="https://radiomanobras.pt">Rádio Manobras</a> e <a href="https://piratona.alg-a.org/">Radio Piratona</a>. Fotografia de <a href="https://flic.kr/p/eU1DMU">James H.</a>.
