[EN] Film-Noir cinematic ambient release by American artist <a href="http://infetu.com/">Infetu</a>: Infiltration in human form. Simulacra, a three track EP revealing the extra-terrestrial encounter (track 1), abduction (track 2), and probe (track 3) through a cinematic soundscape featuring spatters of noise and vocal presence.
