Varia - Magic + Omega
<br /><br />
<img src="https://enoughrecords.scene.org/covers/enrmp269.jpg" />
<br /><br />
[EN] Double album release by <a href="https://variatv.com/">Varia</a>, mashing sampled quotes with electro, hiphop, disco, rock and 80s hits into regurgitated electro disco idm or ambient tracks with it's own <a href="https://variatv.com/variatv.html">online TV channel</a>. Brainchild project of Juho "<a href="https://store.blamstra.in/">Blamstrain</a>" Hietala and Jari "<a href="https://soundcloud.com/jari-pitkanen">1in10</a>" Pitkanen. Two demosceners from Finland active under <a href="https://tpolm.com">TPOLM</a> and <a href="https://mfx.scene.org">MFX</a>. Their sound having already been used in demos such as <a href="https://pouet.net/prod.php?which=54603">Agenda Circling Forth / CNCD & Fairlight</a>.
<br /><br />
[PT] Duplo album pelas mãos do projecto Filandês <a href="https://variatv.com/">Varia</a>, regurgitam excertos samplados com hits de electro, hiphop, disco e rock dos anos 80 transformando o som em novos temas de electro disco idm ou ambient, como pode ser visto no seu próprio <a href="https://variatv.com/variatv.html">canal televisivo online</a>. Projecto da autoria de Juho "<a href="https://store.blamstra.in/">Blamstrain</a>" Hietala e Jari "<a href="https://soundcloud.com/jari-pitkanen">1in10</a>" Pitkanen. Dois demosceners Filandêses activos nos grupos <a href="https://tpolm.com">TPOLM</a> e <a href="https://mfx.scene.org">MFX</a>. O seu som tendo já sedo usado em demos como <a href="https://pouet.net/prod.php?which=54603">Agenda Circling Forth / CNCD & Fairlight</a>.
