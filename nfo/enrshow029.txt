[EN] Enough Records Radio Show, curated by the label's head ps, two hours of sounds taken from the Enough Records eclectic and extensive back-catalogue. Featuring a special mixtape by our artist Jan Robbe (Atomhead, Erratic, Trollhead, Idle Sunder, Duncan Avoid), celebrating his upcoming album "Repercussions" released at Jezgro under his moniker <a href="https://soundcloud.com/janrobbe">Diagnostic</a>. Broadcast at <a href="https://futuremusic.fm/">FutureMusic.FM</a> (9 Dez), <a href="https://radioquantica.com">Rádio Quântica</a> (13 Dez) and <a href="https://radiomanobras.pt">Rádio Manobras</a> (23 Dez). Photo by <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/sloalan/2968044408/">Alan Schmierer</a>.

[PT] Programa de radio Enough Records, curado por ps, cabeçilha regente da editora. Com duas horas de sons retirados do catálogo da Enough. Mixtape especial pelo nosso artista Jan Robbe (Atomhead, Erratic, Trollhead, Idle Sunder, Duncan Avoid), celebrando o lançamento do seu mais recente album "Repercussions" na editora Jezgro sob o nome do seu novo projecto <a href="https://soundcloud.com/janrobbe">Diagnostic</a>. Transmitido na <a href="https://futuremusic.fm/">FutureMusic.FM</a> (9 Dez), <a href="https://radioquantica.com">Rádio Quântica</a> (13 Dez) e <a href="https://radiomanobras.pt">Rádio Manobras</a> (23 Dez).
