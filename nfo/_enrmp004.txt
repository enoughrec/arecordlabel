Skyfusion - Lorelei [enrmp004]

[EN] Laidback electronica EP by <a href="http://www.atonal.org/~skyfusion/" target=_blank>Skyfusion</a>, latest project of Douglas H. Cole. An artist from overseas, with his roots deep in the amiga tracking scene, that had his own DJ spot on enoughradio back in the days. Cover artwork added in 2017, made by <a href="https://www.behance.net/helder-costa">Hélder Costa</a>.
