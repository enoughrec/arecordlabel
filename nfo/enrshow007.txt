[EN] Enough Records Radio Show, curated by the label's head ps, one hour of sounds from the Enough Records eclectic and extensive back-catalogue. Featuring occasional guest mixtapes and random hidden gems from our artists. Broadcast first at <a href="https://futuremusic.fm/">FutureMusic.FM</a> on 10 of December 2017. Photo by <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/sanfranannie/4771824769/">Ann Larie Valentine</a>. Re-broadcast at <a href="https://radiomanobras.pt">Radio Manobras</a> with Portuguese voice over on 7 of January 2018 (<a href="https://www.mixcloud.com/enoughrecords/enough-records-radio-show-007-pt/">mixcloud link</a>).
