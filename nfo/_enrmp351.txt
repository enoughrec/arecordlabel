Seazo - A Tribute To Cosmos [enrmp351]

[EN] We found glitch ambient Norwegian project <a href="https://soundcloud.com/seazo">Seazo</a> by hearing his work <a href="http://www.pouet.net/prod.php?which=62311">on a demo by Cocoon</a>. Loved the track, got in touch, and he was happy to send us this amazing new album titled "A Tribute To Cosmos" for release through Enough Records. Cover artwork is courtesy of Andreas S.

Como adoramos o tema, entramos em contacto com o projecto que se disponibilizou a editar o seu novo album "A Tribute To Cosmos" através da Enough Records. Grafismo cortesia de Andreas S.
