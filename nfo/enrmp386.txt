[EN] Canadian goth rock project <a href="https://thesilenceindustry.bandcamp.com/">The Silence Industry</a> is revealing their ~/.tsi/ computer folder. An hidden album of 6 tracks arranged through 2015 and 2016 in their unmistakable goth rock style with a touch of psychedelic and postpunk references.
