[EN] Split edition between Palaeolithic Doom band <a href="https://www.myspace.com/josueosalvador">Josué, o Salvador em Busca da Perdição</a> and the slow placed sonic behemoth named <a href="https://www.myspace.com/profanmusic">Profan</a>.
<br><br>
Both bands present one track each, consisting in almost 40 minutes of material of the finest doom, sludge and ambient music made in Portugal. Artwork by <a href="https://homemdopacote.wordpress.com/">André Coelho</a>
<br><br>
This edition is also available in limited edition copies of 50 CDR (self-released, currently available at Louie Louie and Lost Underground in Porto) and 100 K7 (released in colaboration with <a href="https://www.bubonicprod.pt.vu/">Bubonic Records</a>). Get your copy!
