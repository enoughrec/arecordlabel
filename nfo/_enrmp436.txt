A.J. Kaufmann - TN-237m [enrmp436]

[EN] With a very wide mix of influences ranging from post-punk, deathrock, industrial, space rock, lofi experimental electronics and krautrock we present to you Polish author and poet <a href="https://ajkaufmann.wordpress.com/">A.J. Kaufmann</a> with his latest full album release of his own unique music style, self-refered as brutalist rock. Artwork by <a href="https://www.justinjackley.com/">Justin Jackley</a>. Album concept based loosely on <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Level_7_(novel)">Level 7</a>, a 1959 science fiction novel by Ukrainian-born Israeli writer Mordecai Roshwald.

[PT] Misturando diversas influências incluindo o post-punk, deathrock, industrial, space rock, electrónica lofi experimental e o krautrock, apresentamos o autor e poeta Polaco <a href="https://ajkaufmann.wordpress.com/">A.J. Kaufmann</a> e o seu mais recente album produzido no seu estilo único, auto-referênciado como rock brutalista. Arte gráfica de <a href="https://www.justinjackley.com/">Justin Jackley</a>. Conceito do album é baseado vagamente na história de ficção cientifica <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Level_7_(novel)">Level 7</a> de Mordecai Roshwald.
