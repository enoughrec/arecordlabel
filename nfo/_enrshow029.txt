ps - Enough Records Radio Show #029 [enrshow029]

[EN] Enough Records Radio Show, curated by the label's head ps, two hours of sounds taken from the Enough Records eclectic and extensive back-catalogue. Featuring a special mixtape by our artist Jan Robbe (Atomhead, Erratic, Trollhead, Idle Sunder, Duncan Avoid), celebrating his upcoming album "Repercussions" released at Jezgro under his moniker <a href="https://soundcloud.com/janrobbe">Diagnostic</a>. Broadcast at <a href="https://futuremusic.fm/">FutureMusic.FM</a> (9 Dez), <a href="https://radioquantica.com">Rádio Quântica</a> (13 Dez) and <a href="https://radiomanobras.pt">Rádio Manobras</a> (23 Dez).
