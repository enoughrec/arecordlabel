[EN] <a href="http://www.entity.be/duncanavoid/">Duncan Avoid</a> is a cooperation project from the founding fathers of our friend netlabel <a href="http://www.entity.be/" target=_blank>Entity Records</a> from Belgium. This album is whole about cyberpunk culture, transhumanism, reflection on cybernetic implants and it's consequences to our future society. Artwork by <a href="http://www.sparth.com/">Nicolas Bouvier</a>. Remixes by No Xivic, Danny Kreutzfeld and ps.
<br /><br />
[PT] <a href="http://www.entity.be/duncanavoid/">Duncan Avoid</a> é um projecto colaborativo dos fundadores da netlabel Belga <a href="http://www.entity.be/" target=_blank>Entity Records</a>. Apresentam-se com um album dedicado à cultura cyberpunk, reflectindo sobre o transhumanismo, os implantes cibernéticos e as suas consequências na sociedade futura. Arte gráfica de <a href="http://www.sparth.com/">Nicolas Bouvier</a>.
