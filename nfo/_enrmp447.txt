António Bizarro - City of Industry: Cellar Door [enrmp447]

[EN] Electronic industrial album with cinematic references and influences from metal and techno sounds, put together by Portuguese project <a href="http://androidapocalypse.blogspot.pt/">António Bizarro</a>, a follow up trilogy release to Slow Gun and Cruel Devices released in 2018. Artwork by Sérgio Palma Agostinho.

[PT] Album de ambiental industrial com influências de metal e tecno, produzido por <a href="http://androidapocalypse.blogspot.pt/">António Bizarro</a>, encerrando a trilogia de edições do projecto City of Industry após os albums Slow Gun e Cruel Devices editados em 2018. Grafismo de Sérgio Palma Agostinho.
