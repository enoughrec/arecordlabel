M-PeX - Odysseia [enrmp364]

[EN] Latest album by <a href="https://soundcloud.com/mpex">M-PeX</a>, his 5th on Enough Records is a double album. It draws it's inspirations from two odysseys: Homero's and Kubrik's. The first cd, sub-titled "Prologus" is entirely acoustic, with M-PeX on the Portuguese guitar and André Coelho on double bass. The second cd is titled "Epilogus", adding electronic elements to the sounds and featuring additional guest artist <a href="https://www.facebook.com/xactodj">DJ X-Acto</a> providing some additional electronics and scratch sounds. Artwork by <a href="https://www.mozo.pt">Marco Madruga</a>.

[PT] Mais recente album de <a href="https://soundcloud.com/mpex">M-PeX</a>, o seu quinto lançado pela Enough é um album duplo. Inspirado em duas odisseias, a de Homero e a de Kubrik. A primeira parte entitula-se "Prologus" e consiste em 6 temas completamente acústicos, com M-PeX na guitarra portuguesa e André Coelho no contra-baixo. A segunda parte entitula-se "Epilogus" e acrescenta elementos electrónicos com a colaboração do artista convidado <a href="https://www.facebook.com/xactodj">DJ X-Acto</a>. Grafismo de <a href="https://www.mozo.pt">Marco Madruga</a>.
