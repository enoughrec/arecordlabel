[EN] New album of Contaminado gathering some old tracks of acoustic guitar with bossa nova / blues / jazz / world fusion influences and a couple of acappella tracks reminiscent of his previous release at Enough Records. Contaminado is the music project of <a href="https://www.patreon.com/ascmenow">André Coelho</a>. Tracks remastered by <a href="https://www.facebook.com/M-PeX-126605314037429">M-PeX</a>. Artwork by <a href="http://www.wray-co.com/">Amanda Wray</a>.

[PT] Novo album de Contaminado agrupando alguns temas de guitarra acústica com influências de bossa nova / blues / jazz / músicas do mundo, e alguns temas em acappella reminescentes do prévio trabalho lançado na Enough Records. Contaminado é o projecto musical da <a href="https://www.patreon.com/ascmenow">André Coelho</a>. Temas remasterizados por <a href="https://www.facebook.com/M-PeX-126605314037429">M-PeX</a>.
