[EN] Short EP christmas gift by Dream Metaphor, including instrumental versions of two tracks taken from their latest album release <a href="https://dreammetaphor.bandcamp.com/album/micro-ambi-ncias">Micro Ambiências</a> available as limited edition physical album with sounds from their live performance that took place on the December 21st, the proceeds of that album and performance support a local society that helps kids in the city of Gulpilhares. Artwork by <a href="https://cargocollective.com/helder-costa">Hélder Costa</a>.
<br /><br />
[PT] Pequena prenda de natal de Dream Metaphor com dois temas em versão instrumental retirados do album <a href="https://dreammetaphor.bandcamp.com/album/micro-ambi-ncias">Micro Ambiências</a> recentemente editado em CD, limitado a 300 cópias. Album que surge no seguimento da performance dada dia 21 de Dezembro 2018, receitas do album e performance revertem a favor d'A Aldeia de Crianças SOS de Gulpilhares. Grafismo de <a href="https://cargocollective.com/helder-costa">Hélder Costa</a>.
