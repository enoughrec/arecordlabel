[EN] From Niagara Falls, Canada, Jesse Williams (also known as fast idio), brings us a lush idm track called "he made me eat grass" mixing crunchy beats with a japanese koto driven melody. This track was originally released in September 2002 as a standalone track and then later re-released under our current catalogue numbering. Re-released in 2012 with updated cover artwork by <a href="http://www.graphicboost.org/">Dina Barbosa</a>.
