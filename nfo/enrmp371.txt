[EN] 7 years after their original debut on Enough, Portuguese oldschool EBM project U.M.M. is back to the releases with a new EP including a bunch of remixes by friend projects Corrosion, Aktivehate, Sci-Fi Industries, Waste Disposal Machine, A.D.A.C. 8286 and Technomader.
