[EN] We launch the texts section of Enough Records with a short novel in Portuguese by <a href="https://www.patreon.com/ascmenow">André Coelho</a>, set in a future time when the necessities of life are taken care of, where we follow the story of L as she rediscovers herself and her priorities in life.
