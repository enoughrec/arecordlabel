[EN] Enough Records Radio Show, curated by the label's head ps, one hour of sounds from the Enough Records eclectic and extensive back-catalogue. Featuring occasional guest mixtapes and random hidden gems from our artists. Broadcast first at <a href="https://futuremusic.fm/">FutureMusic.FM</a> on 26 of November 2017. Photo by <a href="https://flic.kr/p/76Xc98">Jonas Bengtsson</a>. Re-broadcast at <a href="https://radiomanobras.pt">Radio Manobras</a> with Portuguese voice over on 24 of December 2017 (<a href="https://www.mixcloud.com/enoughrecords/enough-records-radio-show-006-pt/">mixcloud link</a>).
