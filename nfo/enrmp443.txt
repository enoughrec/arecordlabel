[EN] New album release from Lithuanian musician <a href="https://www.martinrach.com/">Martin Rach</a>, this time under his Elvii Marten moniker. Dissonant tripstep with IDM and jazz influences, very trippy stuff this third spaceship twisting your sense of space and time.

[PT] Novo album pelas mãos do projecto Lituano <a href="https://www.martinrach.com/">Martin Rach</a>, desta feita sob o nome de Elvii Marten. Tripstep dissonante com influências de IDM e jazz, uma terceira nave espacial que distorce as noções de tempo e espaço.
