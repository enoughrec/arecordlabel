Corruttela - Corruttela [enrmp440]

[EN] Corruttela is an Italian side-project rising from the artistic connection of Emiliano Pietrini (<a href="https://www.facebook.com/Globoscuro1/">Globoscuro</a>) + Edoardo Pistolesi Somigli (<a href="https://www.discogs.com/artist/5385768-Ed%C3%B8-Pist%C3%B8-S%C3%B8mi">Edø Pistø Sømi</a>) with the aim of exploring new sound potentialities through a 4-hands compositional approach in which the two artistic personalities are giving birth to a renovated concept, in which electronic and analogue ambient refined flows are walking arm in arm, and melt in a psychedelic and concrete "harmonious disharmony". The 4 tracks in this first work are characterized by a heterogeneous homogeneity, mainly based on the alchemic fusion of electronic sounds intertwining with smart piano-esque bleeps, gusts of concrete noise, irrational wooden percussions, synthesizers and theremin that walk together with different kind of steps but in a stubbornly common direction. Paradoxically the panorama that opens up raves geometrically outlined visions, it is a sort of sonic poem without words which leads on a corridor with many doors and many entrances that revolve around the different cardinal points of a common caothic universe.

[PT] Corruttela é um projecto Italiano do duo Emiliano Pietrini (<a href="https://www.facebook.com/Globoscuro1/">Globoscuro</a>) e Edoardo Pistolesi Somigli (<a href="https://www.discogs.com/artist/5385768-Ed%C3%B8-Pist%C3%B8-S%C3%B8mi">Edø Pistø Sømi</a>) com o objectivo de explorar novas sonoridades a 4 mãos, duas personalidades distintas a misturar o electrónico e o analógico com uma homogeneidade heterogénica do uso de toques de piano, rajadas de barulho sólido, percursões de madeira irracionais, sintetizadores, theremin. Á descoberta de uma passagem de poema sonoro no universo caótico comum.
