dUASsEMIcOLCHEIASiNVERTIDAS / Parpar - dSCi / Parpar Split [enrmp430]

[EN] Split vinyl album between long time Enough Records collaborators <a href="https://duassemicolcheiasinvertidas.bandcamp.com/">dSCi</a> and the duo <a href="https://www.facebook.com/parparduo/">Parpar</a>, two Portuguese projects sharing tours and a sensibility for the punk-rock free jazz type of sounds. This album was recorded, mixed and mastered by Milo Gomberoff at <a href="https://hukotaudio.com/">Hukot studio</a> in Barcelona. Cover artwork by <a href="https://krafica.com/">Katafu</a>. A co-release effort by <a href="http://terapiadoruido.pt.vu/">ATR</a>, <a href="https://abesta.bandcamp.com/">A Besta</a>, <a href="https://lepers.bandcamp.com/">Lepers</a>, <a href="http://epicericords.wixsite.com/epicericords">Epicericords</a>, <a href="http://moncul.org/">Et Mon Cul C'est Du Tofu?</a>, <a href="https://experimentaculorecords.bandcamp.com/">Experimentáculo Records</a>, <a href="http://www.gafferrecords.com/">Gaffer Records</a>, <a href="https://pandarecords.bandcamp.com/">Panda Records</a> and Enough Records.

[PT] Split de vinil entre as <a href="https://duassemicolcheiasinvertidas.bandcamp.com/">dUAS sEMIcOLCHEIAS iNVERTIDAS</a> e o duo <a href="https://www.facebook.com/parparduo/">Parpar</a>, 7 temas algures entre o punk-rock e o free jazz. o culminar da sua tourné pela europa com um split gravado e misturado por Milo Gomberoff no <a href="https://hukotaudio.com/">Hukot studio</a> em Barcelona. Grafismo por <a href="https://krafica.com/">Katafu</a>.
