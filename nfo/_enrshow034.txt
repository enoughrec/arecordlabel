ps - Enough Records Radio Show #034 [enrshow034]

[EN] Enough Records Radio Show, curated by ps, two hours of sounds taken from Enough Records eclectic and extensive back-catalogue. This episode premiered on May 2019 and was broadcast at <a href="https://radioquantica.com">Rádio Quântica</a>, <a href="https://radiomanobras.pt">Rádio Manobras</a> and <a href="https://piratona.alg-a.org/">Radio Piratona</a>. On this episode we do a special retrospective show focusing on two compilations co-organized with <a href="https://thisco.bandcamp.com/">Thisco Records</a>.
