[EN] Laidback electronica smelling of Japan. Debut release by The Antti Jädertpolm Quartet, a project by the Finnish ex Barcelona resident and prolific freelance graphics designer <a href="https://tpolm.com/fthr">Antti 'FthrProductions' Jädertpolm</a>. Track 7 in collaboration with <a href="https://kschzt.com/">Kschzt</a>.

[PT] Electronica com cheirinho a Japão. Tema 7 em colaboração com <a href="https://kschzt.com/">Kschzt</a>.
