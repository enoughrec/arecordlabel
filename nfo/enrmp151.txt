Goto80 - Open Funk Sores

[EN] Swedish blipmusic chiptune superstar <a href="http://www.goto80.com/">Goto80</a> attacks us with a handful of folk chiptronica gamemusic soundscapes from the icecream age of doom and terror, best played in random mode. The release also includes the original mod files for the Amiga perverts. Plus a musicdisk version for the Playstation Portable with coding courtesy of <a href="http://titandemo.org/">Titan</a> and graphic animations provided by <a href="http://www.raquelmeyers.com/">Raquel Meyers</a>. <a href="http://enoughrecords.scene.org/goto80_openfunksores/">web browser version</a> and <a href="http://itun.es/i6Dn9yJ">iOS version</a> also available.
<br /><br />
[PT] Mini-album com interface disponivel para a PSP da Sony (cortesia dos <a href="http://titandemo.org/">Titan</a>), pelas mãos do Sueco <a href="http://www.goto80.com/">Goto80</a>, com grafismo da Espanhola <a href="http://www.raquelmeyers.com/">Raquel Meyers</a>. Música 8bit para as massas nostalgicas dos sons dos videojogos. Também se encontra disponivél versão funcional no <a href="http://enoughrecords.scene.org/goto80_openfunksores/">browser</a> e <a href="http://itun.es/i6Dn9yJ">aplicação para dispositivos moveis da Apple</a>.
