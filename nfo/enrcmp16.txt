Various Artists - AtlanThis

[EN] Compilation of experimental electronic music artists from Portugal and the United States, on the theme of travelling across the Atlantic ocean inside a ship cargo container in search of a new world. Organized and co-released by <a href="http://thisco.net/">Thisco Records</a>. Tracks by Low Pressure System, <a href="http://tpolm.org/~ps/">ps</a>, <a href="http://www.myspace.com/structura">Structura</a>, <a href="http://www.sicktothebackteeth.com/">Sick To The Back Teeth</a>, <a href="http://infetu.com/">Infetu</a>, <a href="http://www.mystifiedmusic.com/">Mystified</a>, <a href="http://www.myspace.com/traumaticodesmame">Traumático Desmame</a>, <a href="http://www.thisco.net/scifiindustries.htm">Sci-Fi Industries</a>, Wedding Noise and <a href="http://www.thermidor-project.net/">Thermidor</a>.
<br /><br />
[PT] Compilação de artistas Portuguêses e Norte Americanos com temas de electrónica experimental sobre o tema de atravessar o oceano Atlântico em busca do mundo novo dentro de um contentor de carga. Organizado e co-editado pela <a href="http://thisco.net/">Thisco Records</a>. Temas de Low Pressure System, <a href="http://tpolm.org/~ps/">ps</a>, <a href="http://www.myspace.com/structura">Structura</a>, <a href="http://www.sicktothebackteeth.com/">Sick To The Back Teeth</a>, <a href="http://infetu.com/">Infetu</a>, <a href="http://www.mystifiedmusic.com/">Mystified</a>, <a href="http://www.myspace.com/traumaticodesmame">Traumático Desmame</a>, <a href="http://www.thisco.net/scifiindustries.htm">Sci-Fi Industries</a>, Wedding Noise and <a href="http://www.thermidor-project.net/">Thermidor</a>.
