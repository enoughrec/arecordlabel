[EN] Electronic industrial album by Portuguese project <a href="http://androidapocalypse.blogspot.pt/">António Bizarro</a>, also a dystopic science fiction writer who recently released a trilog of books through coolbooks. This album gathers 12 tracked from his old moniker City of Industry, and re-releases them free for download on Enough Records. Artwork by Sérgio Palma Agostinho.
<br /><br />
[PT] Album de electrónica industrial pelas mãos de <a href="http://androidapocalypse.blogspot.pt/">António Bizarro</a>, músico e escritor de cenários de ficção cientifica distópica tais como a recente editada trilogia de livros pela coolbooks. Este album junta 12 temas do seu antigo projecto City of Industry, re-editando-as na Enough Records com arte de Sérgio Palma Agostinho.
