[EN] 7 track dark electro / ebm EP originally released back in 2006 by Portuguese darkwave / electronic project <a href="https://inperfektion.bandcamp.com/">In[Perfektion]</a> (featuring a remix by <a href="https://www.edensyntheticcorps.com/">ESC</a>) now remastered and available free for download as part of the countdown to their upcoming album "Vultures Among Us", available through <a href="https://www.hesperus-records.com">Hesperus Records</a>.

[PT] Re-edição re-masterizada de um EP originalmente editado em cdr edição limitada pelo projecto de darkwave / futurepop Português <a href="https://inperfektion.bandcamp.com/">In[Perfektion]</a>. Parte da contagem decrescente para o lançamento do seu novo album "Vultures Among Us", disponivel pela <a href="https://www.hesperus-records.com">Hesperus Records</a>.
