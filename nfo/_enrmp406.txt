Grid Resistor - Beta [enrmp406]

[EN] Beta is the second of a series of releases by Grid Resistor, the new project of Thomas Park (also known as <a href="https://archive.org/details/ThomasParkBenchmarkHub">Mystified</a> or Mister Vapor). Grid Resistor focuses on using field recordings of machines, weaving post-industrial ambient sounds. Best consumed with headphones. Photo by <a href="http://tinyurl.com/yazclokm">Jimmy Gunawan</a>.

[PT] Beta é a segunda edição de uma série de albums de Grid Resistor, o novo projecto de Thomas Park (<a href="https://archive.org/details/ThomasParkBenchmarkHub">Mystified</a>, Mister Vapor). Grid Resistor foca-se no uso de gravações de campo de máquinas para criar um ambient pós industrial. Recomenda-se o uso de headphones. Fotografia de <a href="http://tinyurl.com/yazclokm">Jimmy Gunawan</a>.
