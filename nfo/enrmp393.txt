Jared C. Balogh - A Change Equals A Rest
<br /><br />
<img src="http://enoughrecords.scene.org/covers/enrmp393.jpg" />
<br /><br />
[EN] Album by Jared C. Balogh, pushing his jazz and avant-garde influences to a more classical composition album. The tracks invoke the vulnerable uncertainty of falling in love again, inner doubt, introspective reflection, expectations of wanting to connect with someone and being afraid of taking the next step.
<br /><br />
[PT] Album de Jared C. Balogh puxando as usas influências jazz e avant-garde num album de composição clássica. Os temas invocam a vulnerabilidade e incerteza de se estar a apaixonar, dúvida interna, reflexão introspectiva, espectativas de se querer ligar mas algum medo de dar o próximo passo.
