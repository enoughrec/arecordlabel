[EN] Meso was born on the island of Lewis off north-west Scotland and is currently living in Glasgow. He's been making music for over 8 or 9 years in varying shapes and forms and now brings us this Glaschu EP, 4 tracks made over the course of 2-3 years in Lewis and Glasgow with the main idea: warm, emotional electronic music. You can hear more of his work at <a href="http://www.myspace.com/meso01">his myspace site</a>.
<br /><br />
[PT] EP de electrónica quente e emocional pelas mãos de <a href="http://www.myspace.com/meso01">Meso</a>, projecto oriundo do norte da Escócia.
