Frame - Visions [enrmp224]

[EN] Originally self-released back in 2006 as a limited edition cdr, Portuguese project <a href="https://www.framemusic.org/" rel="nofollow">Frame</a> brings us a remastered version of his Visions album, now re-released in 2009 with new artwork, courtesy of our friend <a href="https://www.myspace.com/unreasonablebehaviour" rel="nofollow">Hélder Costa</a>. Laidback chillout electronica with some drum and bass and trance influences.

[PT] Originalmente lançado em 2006 como edição de autor em formato cdr, o projecto Português <a href="https://www.framemusic.org/" rel="nofollow">Frame</a> apresenta-nos em 2009 esta versão remasterizada. Com nova arte gráfica cortesia do nosso amigo designer gráfico <a href="https://www.myspace.com/unreasonablebehaviour" rel="nofollow">Hélder Costa</a>. Electrónica chill out com muitas influências do trance e drum n bass.
