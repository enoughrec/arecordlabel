Martin Rach - Unfinished Loops [enrmp435]

[EN] Latest album from long term Enough Records collaborator <a href="http://www.martinrach.com/">Martin Rach</a> from Lithuania, exploring some Unfinished Loops in his usual tripstep jazz influenced way. Expect eerie settings, deep wobbling bass sounds, cinematic constructs and strong silences dominating the structure of the tracks. Artwork by Martin Rach and ps.

[PT] Album mais recente de um colaborador recurrente da Enough Records <a href="http://www.martinrach.com/">Martin Rach</a> da Lituânia, explorando em Unfinished Loops os sesu tipicos padrões de tripstep com influências do jazz. Esperem ambientes sombrios, baixos dominantes, construções cinematográficas e silêncios dominantes na estrutura. Grafismo por Martin Rach e ps.
